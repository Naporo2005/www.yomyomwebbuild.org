About us

Welcome to You Go Feel Am Fitness.


We are a high-energy fitness center based in Tamale, Ghana. We focus on helping people build strength, improve their health, and transform their bodies through effective and structured training. We specialize in creating a motivating workout environment that doesn't just push you physically, but also helps you build discipline, confidence, and consistency.


Our programs include strength training, fat loss workouts, and general fitness coaching designed for all levels—from beginners to advanced athletes. We also provide practical guidance on nutrition, water intake, and healthy lifestyle habits to support real results.
