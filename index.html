Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Yomyom WebBuild?

Go to the Templates section, pick a design you like, and click "Use this template." Follow the steps from there — our team will guide you through customization, confirmation, and delivery.

How long does delivery take?

After payment is confirmed, most websites are delivered within 2–5 business days depending on complexity and requested customizations.

Can Yomyom WebBuild handle my hosting?

Yes! You can either receive the website files to self-host, or request our team to handle hosting and setup on your behalf. Pricing varies — contact us for details.

What payment methods do you accept?

Payment options are confirmed during the WhatsApp order confirmation step. We'll walk you through accepted methods based on your region.
