Why Yomyom Academy

A school built differently.

We don't measure success only in grades. We measure it in confident, curious, kind young people who are ready for whatever comes next.

Academic Excellence

Our curriculum is rigorous, internationally benchmarked, and designed to challenge every student at their own level — from foundational literacy to advanced placement.

Small Class Sizes

With a 1:14 teacher-to-student ratio, no child gets lost. Every student is known by name, supported personally, and encouraged to reach further than they thought possible.

Character First

Kindness, resilience, and integrity are taught alongside algebra and grammar. We prepare students for life, not just exams.

Global Perspective

From Model UN to language exchange programmes, students graduate with a broad view of the world and the empathy to navigate it.

Arts & Creativity

Music, visual arts, drama, and design thinking are woven into every year group — because creativity sharpens every other kind of thinking.

Sport & Wellbeing

Physical education, mindfulness, and team sports develop healthy habits and emotional regulation alongside athletic confidence.

Admissions

Joining Yomyom Academy is straightforward.

We welcome applications year-round for available places. Our admissions team reviews every application personally — no child is just a number.

1

Submit an Application

Fill in the online enquiry form or drop in to our office. Bring previous school reports if available.

2

Informal Assessment

A friendly, low-pressure session with our team so we can understand your child's strengths, learning style, and any support needs.

3

Campus Visit

We invite families for a guided tour so your child can see the school, meet students, and ask their own questions.

4

Offer & Enrolment

Once a place is confirmed, we send a welcome pack and assign a buddy — so your child has a friend waiting on day one.
